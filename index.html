Use your device as your Thinkfarm wallet. You can charge it in cash.

There are two separate accounts for coffee and cold drinks like mate, cola and beer. Simply click the buttons and follow the instructions.

After some money is on your account, you can buy coffee or cold drinks, depending which account is filled.

Bookmark this page for easy access later in the kitchen or even put an icon on your home screen.
